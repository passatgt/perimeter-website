Perimeter was inspired by my brother, a landscaper, who needed help calculating areas for material estimates. I realized how difficult it could be to measure irregularly shaped spaces. Perimeter uses AR technology to make accurate perimeter and area calculations easy.

The idea of the app came to me after my borther - who is a landscaper - asked me to help him measure out various areas so he could calculate the material he needs to do the job. Since it was not a simple rectangle, it was actually pretty hard to get an accurate measurement.

So I started to developer Perimeter, which uses AR to map out the area and calculate precise perimeter and area measurements. It can also create a branded PDF blueprint, which can printed and annotated.
