How accurate this is?

Perimeter uses advanced Augmented Reality (AR) technology to deliver highly accurate measurements, typically within a 1-2% difference of actual distances. While the app is designed to provide precision for most outdoor projects, factors such as lighting conditions, the surface of the area being measured, and the steadiness of your movements can affect accuracy. For best results, I recommend using Perimeter in well-lit conditions and on clear, flat surfaces. If theres a large elevation change in the area, I recommend scanning multiple smaller areas. Always double-check measurements for critical projects, especially if exact precision is crucial.

Can i use it indoors?

You can, but Perimeter is designed to be used to measure larger outdoor areas, so it's an ideal tool for gardeners, landscapers, general conractors, concrete companies and more.

Is there a subscription fee?

No, the app is free to download. To save your measurements and blueprints, you can upgrade to the PRO version with a single lifetime in-app purchase.
